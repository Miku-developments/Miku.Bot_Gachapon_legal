Miku.Bot is designed to help with **automated image moderation**.

The Bot may:

* Scan uploaded images (e.g., PNG, JPG, and similar formats) using OCR (Optical Character Recognition).
* Detect text within images.
* Compare detected text against server guidelines or configured banned words.
* Automatically remove violating images.
* Notify moderators when violations are detected.

You understand that automated moderation may not be perfect and can produce false positives or false negatives.

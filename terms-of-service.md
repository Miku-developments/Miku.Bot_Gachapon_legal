## 2. Purpose of the Bot

Miku.N is a companion utility for the **Gachapon** Discord card game. Its primary features include:

* **Gachapon drop detection:** Monitors messages from the Gachapon bot (user ID `815289915557675118`) that match a card-drop pattern.
* **Local OCR:** Downloads drop images and extracts character names, series, rarity/global-wishlist values, and print IDs using a local Tesseract OCR engine.
* **Wishlist pings:** Matches extracted card info against a global wishlist database and pings users who have wished those cards.
* **Statistics & missions:** Displays aggregate drop/scan statistics and weekly/daily mission progress.
* **Optional token / premium system:** Lets users and servers unlock additional scanning capacity (e.g. 3- and 4-card drops) via a token balance or premium status, including monthly plans.

Miku.N is **not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by the Gachapon bot or its developers**. "Gachapon" is referenced only as the source of the drop messages the Bot observes.

You understand that OCR-based card recognition is **not perfect** and can produce incorrect matches, missed pings, or false positives. You use the Bot at your own discretion.
